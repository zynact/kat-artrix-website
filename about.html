About Us

Company Overview

We are providing best in class indutrial solutions for our customers since 2018.

KAT & ARTRIX Technologies is a pioneering engineering company specializing in bespoke and turnkey assembly and testing solutions. Founded in 2018 with a focus on design consulting and PLC programming, we expanded into machine building in 2024. Our team of experienced professionals excels in various domains of machine design and manufacturing, allowing us to deliver innovative solutions across multiple industries including Automobile, Energy, Medical, and Food Processing sectors.

Our Mission


Our mission is to deliver tailored, high-quality automation and machinery solutions that enhance our clients' productivity, efficiency, and competitiveness.
